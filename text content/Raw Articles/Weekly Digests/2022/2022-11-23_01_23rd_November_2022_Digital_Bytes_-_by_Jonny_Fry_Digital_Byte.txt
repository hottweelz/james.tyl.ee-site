TITLE: The potential role of blockchains in capital markets

to facilitate the massive cross-border movement of capital, capital markets have evolved into a worldwide linked web of banks and intermediaries. Regulation, technology-led market disruption and basic business economics are transforming the capital markets. Blockchain could potentially eliminate intermediaries in capital markets, and so improve the efficiency of the market infrastructure.
