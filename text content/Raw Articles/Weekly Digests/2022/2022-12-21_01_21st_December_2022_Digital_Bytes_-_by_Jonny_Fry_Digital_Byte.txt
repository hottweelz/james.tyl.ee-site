TITLE: Blockchains are changing the way you buy your home

blockchain technology has already been successfully used for a number of years in Australia by a company called PEXA, which has recorded over 11 million property transactions. The same firm has recently set its sights on the UK to revolutionise the way in which mortgages are handled and processed. Digital currencies now offer those involved in buying or selling a property an increasing range of options to pay for real estate and avoid many of current costs - potentially much to the chagrin of existing lenders and intermediaries in the property sector.
