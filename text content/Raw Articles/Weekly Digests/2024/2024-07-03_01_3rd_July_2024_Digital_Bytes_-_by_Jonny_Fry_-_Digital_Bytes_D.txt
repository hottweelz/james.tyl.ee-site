TITLE: Making micropayments using cryptocurrencies and stablecoins

micropayments using stablecoins and cryptocurrencies are poised to revolutionise the creative economy by enabling low-cost, efficient transactions. This democratises revenue, allowing more artists to earn sustainable incomes - even with smaller audiences - as well as reducing reliance on traditional channels. Blockchain-based systems improve data transparency and control, so aiding content customisation. Increasingly, the programmability of digital currencies is being recognised, enabling payments in ways that traditional fiat currencies are unable to do.
