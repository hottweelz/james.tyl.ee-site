TITLE: Stablecoins: the ‘killer app’ of the financial world? (Part 1)

stablecoins are revolutionising global finance by offering fast, low-cost, programmable transactions that bridge traditional systems and DeFi. They enhance financial inclusion, streamline remittances and drive efficiency in settlements and FX markets, so challenging legacy infrastructure. However, their rise raises questions about regulation, adoption and their potential to replace traditional payment rails. Could stablecoins become the foundation of a more inclusive, efficient financial future and, indeed, be a ‘killer app’ for the financial sector?
