TITLE: Decentralized autonomous universities: redefining education governance - in recent years, the intersection of blockchain technology and education has spurred those inative concepts that challenge traditional models of governance and administration. One such groundbreaking idea is the notion of

decentralized autonomous universities (DAUs). These institutions leverage blockchain's decentralized nature to create autonomous, transparent and democratic systems for managing academic operations. This article delves into the concept of DAUs and explores their potential to revolutionise education governance.
