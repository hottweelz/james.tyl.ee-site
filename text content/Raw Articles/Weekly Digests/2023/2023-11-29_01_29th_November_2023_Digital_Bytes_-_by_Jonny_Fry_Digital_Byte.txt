TITLE: Assets managers: digitize or die? (part 1)

the evolution of collective investments to become digitized seems to be increasingly inevitable. This article considers how collective investments schemes have developed and evolved to become a substantial industry which impacts on savers and pensioners worldwide. The need to adhere to regulatory demands and AI's growing role in wealth management will further pressurise asset managers to adopt digitization (despite prevalent archaic practices), or die.
