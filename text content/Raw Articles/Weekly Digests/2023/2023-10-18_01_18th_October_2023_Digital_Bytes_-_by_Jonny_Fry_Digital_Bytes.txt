TITLE: Web3 in banking: digitize or die?

as we live in the age of Web3 and the metaverse, we are seeing the development of new technologies and business models that could change how banks operate. When it comes to business, Web3 has significant effects on everyone involved; better services for end users such as regular banking, investment bankers and companies and individuals looking for alternative banking providers and solutions.
