TITLE: Blockchain technology can help to free capital tied up in your home

blockchain technology is being used in a variety of ways to help those homeowners who are often capital rich but income poor gain access to capital. Residential real estate prices have substantially increased in the last twelve years and now more and more homeowners are looking at ways to unlock the capital tied up in their homes. Blockchain-powered solutions could offer the promise of shared ownership, whereby enabling smaller investors to gain a step onto the property ladder whilst unlocking capital tied up in homeowners’ properties.
