TITLE: Introducing the Commissioner for digital money: safeguarding privacy and rights in the age of programmable money - The global race to develop Central Bank Digital Currencies (CBDCs) and Stablecoins has quietly created unprecedented challenges across the world. The intrinsic programmability of this money and the oceans of new and valuable centralised data produced daily create chronic as well as acute governance challenges in each and every jurisdiction. A gnawing temptation to governments and other agencies to abuse these new powers. Both at implementation and then again over the longer term.

Do we therefore need a Commissioner for Digital Money, similar to the UK’s ICO , for the digital pound and Sterling based stablecoins?
