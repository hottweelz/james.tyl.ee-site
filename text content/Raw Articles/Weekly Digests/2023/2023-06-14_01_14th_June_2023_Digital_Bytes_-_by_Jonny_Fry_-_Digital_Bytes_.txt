TITLE: Gender inequality in the metaverse

the number of women employed in the information technology (IT) industry has never been evenly distributed; and the metaverse does not need to follow this pattern either . Citibank believes that the metaverse could be utilised by 5.3billion people by 2030 and is potentially a $8-$13trillion opportunity. The metaverse indeed has the potential to significantly impact the global economy and give everyone who uses it new and more equal opportunities.
