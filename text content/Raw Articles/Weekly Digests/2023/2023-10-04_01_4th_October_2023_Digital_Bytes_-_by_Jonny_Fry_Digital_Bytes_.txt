TITLE: 4th ober 2023

Transforming the automotive landscape: Mercedes-Benz's blockchain-powered journey - Mercedes-Benz is embracing blockchain technology to enhance supply chain transparency and so revolutionize various aspects of the automotive industry, from supply chain management and vehicle monitoring to payments and digital art. Its innovations include in-car fingerprint payments, NFT collections and a blockchain-driven digital currency for eco-friendly driving. Mercedes-Benz is at the forefront of automotive blockchain adoption, pioneering transformative solutions that redefine the future of mobility and digital art. It is also teaming up with Mastercard to drive blockchain adoption.
