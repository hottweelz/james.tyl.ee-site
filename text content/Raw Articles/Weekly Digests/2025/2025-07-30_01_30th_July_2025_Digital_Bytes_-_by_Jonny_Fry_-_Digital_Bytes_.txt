TITLE: Tokenised assets and the future of investment: real estate, equities and beyond

tokenised assets are reshaping global finance by making investments in real estate, equities and bonds more accessible through blockchain technology. Platforms such as RealT, Franklin Templeton and JP Morgan demonstrate real-world use cases. Benefits include fractional ownership, 24/7 trading and reduced settlement times, together with regulatory developments by the FCA and SEC aiming to support investor protection. With blockchains such asEthereum, Polygon and Avalanche driving adoption, tokenisation could unlock trillions in idle capital from real estate.
