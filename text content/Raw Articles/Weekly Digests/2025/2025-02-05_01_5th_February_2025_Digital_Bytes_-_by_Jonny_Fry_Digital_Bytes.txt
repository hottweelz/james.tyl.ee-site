NFTs’ impact on scientific research and funding - NFTs are transforming scientific research by enabling secure ownership, funding and data sharing through blockchain technology. They allow researchers to tokenise intellectual property, streamline licensing and create decentralised funding models, whereby reducing reliance on traditional grants. As decentralised science (DeSci) evolves, NFTs are poised to revolutionise how research is funded, shared and monetised - potentially democratising access to knowledge and innovation.
