AI, blockchain and the future of asset management: disrupting markets or re-inventing finance? - AI is reshaping asset management by enhancing decision-making efficiency and accuracy. Machine learning models analyse financial data, anticipate asset movements and optimise portfolio management, whereby redefining investment strategies. The convergence of AI and blockchain, exemplified by Virtuals Protocol, introduces AI into decentralised finance together with the rise of AI-driven cryptocurrencies and tokenised assets pointing to an increasingly automated and decentralised financial landscape.
