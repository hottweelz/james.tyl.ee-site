TITLE: The memecoin ecosystem: a deep dive into internet culture meets crypto speculation

the memecoin ecosystem blends internet culture, speculative trading and community dynamics into a unique corner of the crypto market whereby evolving into a multi-billion-dollar phenomenon. Memecoins such as Shiba Inu, PEPE and WIF thrive on viral appeal, driven by memes and online sentiment rather than technical innovation. Communities form around these coins, often fuelled by humour and shared narratives. But despite their simplicity and speculative nature, memecoins serve as gateways for newcomers to the broader crypto world, reflecting the emotional and cultural side of Web3. However, the market is also rife with scams and volatility.
