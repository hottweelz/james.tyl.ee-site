Pix and UPI payment success stories: what lessons can stablecoins and tokenised deposits learn? -Pix in Brazil and UPI in India are leading examples of successful instant payment systems (IPSs) that have transformed their respective payment landscapes. Both systems prioritise speed, low cost and accessibility (so driving financial inclusion and reducing reliance on cash) and offer valuable lessons for stablecoins and tokenised deposits. These digital assets could benefit from IPS features such as regulatory compliance, interoperability, security and scalability to enhance trust and adoption. The success of Pix and UPI highlights the potential for IPSs to modernise financial ecosystems worldwide.
