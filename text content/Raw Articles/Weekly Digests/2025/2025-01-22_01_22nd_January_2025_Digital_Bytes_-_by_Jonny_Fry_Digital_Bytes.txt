TITLE: Digital investing on the rise: how the way we buy assets has changed

investing has evolved from reliance on brokers to a tech-driven, democratised landscape fuelled by AI, blockchain and tokenisation. Retail investors now access exclusive assets such as real estate for as little as $50, whilst robo-advisors and gamified platforms empower younger generations. And social media's influence has also sparked impulsive, FOMO-driven decisions - though often with regretful outcomes. Meanwhile, DeFi is removing intermediaries and VR/AR is enhancing financial literacy. The future of investing hinges on balancing accessibility with responsibility, ensuring innovation benefits all without fuelling financial instability.
