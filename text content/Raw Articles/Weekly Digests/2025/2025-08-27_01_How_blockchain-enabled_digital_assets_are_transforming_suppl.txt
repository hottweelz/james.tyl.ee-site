TITLE: How blockchain-enabled digital assets are transforming supply chains and trade finance

blockchain-enabled digital assets (especially stablecoins, tokenised deposits and real-world asset tokenisation) are transforming supply chains and trade finance. By enabling transparent tracking, instant settlement and automated compliance, they synchronise goods and money flows, reduce costs and foster trust whereby paving the way for a faster, more efficient and interconnected global economy.
