How Bitcoin can enable EU citizenship: legal loopholes and golden visas - Bitcoin is increasingly enabling wealthy investors to gain EU residency and citizenship by funding “golden visa” programs, although only after converting crypto into fiat and passing strict documentation checks. Whilst no country accepts Bitcoin directly for investment migration, nations such as Portugal, Greece and Austria allow crypto-funded applications through regulated fiat channels. As tax regimes tighten globally, Bitcoin is becoming a tool for legal mobility whereby turning digital wealth into real-world freedom.
