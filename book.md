# Books by James Tylee

This page is the central books hub on james.tyl.ee. It groups the current catalog and links to a dedicated page for each title.

## Current Titles

### The Digital Assets Revolution

**Subtitle:** *A First-Person Journey Through the Future of Money*
**Type:** Upcoming nonfiction
**Page:** https://james.tyl.ee/digital-assets-revolution
**Markdown:** https://james.tyl.ee/digital-assets-revolution.md

James Tylee combines three decades on Wall Street with disability-informed systems thinking to challenge how financial infrastructure, stablecoins, and digital assets should be built. The central thesis: if you build for the person with constraints, you end up with a system that works better for everyone.

### The Bitter Leaf

**Type:** Literary novel
**Page:** https://james.tyl.ee/the-bitter-leaf
**Markdown:** https://james.tyl.ee/the-bitter-leaf.md

When Mai returns from Hanoi to rural Quảng Nam, she finds her father dying, her brother slipping, and a district official pressing on the family farm. *The Bitter Leaf* is a story about inheritance, silence, land pressure, and what it costs to stay when the soil itself becomes contested.

## Shared Throughline

These books work in different genres, but both are built around systems under pressure. One examines financial infrastructure through the lens of accessibility and constraint. The other examines family, land, and memory in post-Đổi Mới Vietnam.
